# User-Interface-and-User-experience
## User Interface (UI):
User Interface refers to the visual elements through which users interact with a product or service — such as buttons, icons, menus, colors, and layout. It focuses on the look and feel of a digital product.

## User Experience (UX):
User Experience is the overall experience a user has when interacting with a product or service. It includes usability, accessibility, ease of navigation, and the emotional response a user feels. UX is about solving problems and creating meaningful experiences.

## 🧾 Overview

This design project is a **product-centric promotional banner** for New Me’s **End of Season Sale**, aimed at boosting engagement and click-through rates on platforms like Instagram.

🛒 This UI layout mimics:
- A fashion storefront display
- Social media sale announcements
- Visual cues for app redirection

> 📱 *Perfect for Instagram stories, highlights, sale banners, or even inside e-commerce apps!*

---

## 🎨 Theme & Concept

🧠 **Concept**: Make shopping visually fun, intuitive, and scroll-stopping  
🎨 **Color Palette**: Warm muted tones with pastel backgrounds to make the tees pop  
💥 **Callouts**: Bold “End Of Season Sale!!!!” CTA and “go to shop” text for direction  
🧩 **Design Style**: Retro-modern meets pop-culture, with a friendly pixel-art mascot in the center

## ✏️ Creation Process

### 🖼️ 1. Product Curation
I selected stylish t-shirts from the **New Me** brand that appeal to a wide youth demographic.

### 🧱 2. Frame Layout
Each product was placed in a **separate frame** with soft, pastel backgrounds for a clean aesthetic.

### 🗯️ 3. Text & Directional Cues
Simple phrases like “go to shop ->” and a central **“End Of Season Sale!!!!”** badge were added to guide customer focus and tap into urgency.

### 🧸 4. Mascot Branding
A pixel chameleon with the text **“New Me”** was used as a fun, youthful mascot at the center of the composition.
